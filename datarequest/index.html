GPDR Compliance

In May of 2018, the EU voted that websites and other online businesses become transparent about the data they collect such as why the data is collected, how the data is collected and processed, and who has access to this data. It also requires websites, like this one to allow users to request deletion of their data, an archive of their data, or the halting of the data logging.
