THE END OF SUNSET

It is I, Skylar, the developer of Sunset. It has been has been about a year and a half since I started working on this bot. With tons of updates, Sunset was loved by the few that used it. Now, I have to manage a website and a new popular bot, CleverBot. With so much of my time being devoted to CleverBot, I don't have nearly as much time to work on Sunset. So today, I have decided to discontinue the Swiss-Army Knife of Discord Bots, Sunset. Moving forward, I'm going to focus on single-purpose bots; Bots that focus on a single idea. I hope you all can accept this decision and will continue to support my future Discord Bots. Signing off - Skylar McCauley
