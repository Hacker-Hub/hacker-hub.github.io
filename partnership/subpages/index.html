Partnership program for HackerHub

This program is for anyone wanting to show their websites to the world, but can't host their own website. All you need to do is apply through the Google Form.

TheHacker


This form is for anyone willing to join the HackerHub Partnership program.
This program will give you access to your own subpage on http://hacker-hub.github.io/.
There are a few rules you must abide by.
1. No NSFW content allowed. Content being shown must be viewable for General Audiences. If you are unsure if the content you want to use is appropriate please contact me.
2. You are allowed to have a maximum of 2 subpages. No more (obviously less if you want)
3. Your subpage structure must match https://hacker-hub/s/requireddata.txt
4. You must have the snippet of code from https://hacker-hub/s/requireddata.txt
5. please provide valid contact info
