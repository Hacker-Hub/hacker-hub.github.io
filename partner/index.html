This program is for anyone wanting to show their content to the world through HackerHub. We look for original and great projects to promote. Through this program you'll be able to get publicity on this site and other HackerHub Partners. Do note that we will not choose just anyone.

This form is for anyone willing to join the HackerHub Partnership Program.
This program will give you access to your own subpage or subdomain on https://www.hacker-hub.com/.
There are a few rules you must abide by.
1. No NSFW content allowed. All Content must be viewable for General Audiences. If you are unsure if the content you want to use is appropriate please contact me.
2. Your page can be a subdomain (example.hacker-hub.com), subpage (hacker-hub.com/partner/example), or your own website.
3. Make sure your page can be viewable on mobile and desktop (or have seperate mobile and desktop versions)
4. If your website includes resource intensive materials you must provide a visible and transparent disclaimer.
5. No Hate Speech.
